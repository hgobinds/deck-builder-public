# Design Plan: AI Integration Plan

## Template

**File:** `_templates/open-template.pptx`
Follow the open template for all slide masters, layouts, fonts, colours, and logo placement. Do not deviate from the template design system.

## Visual Direction

Text-driven with structured ASCII diagrams as the primary visual element on diagram slides. No stock photography. No decorative imagery. Diagrams should be rendered in a monospace font (Courier New or equivalent) inside a lightly bordered text box, centred on the slide. All other slides follow standard open template text layouts.

## Tone

Confident, clear, and enabling. Not hype. Not fear. Calm and practical.
