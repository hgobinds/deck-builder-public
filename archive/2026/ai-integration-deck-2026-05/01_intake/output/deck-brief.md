## Audience

- **Who**: Consultants and project leads (all seniority levels, organization-wide sharing)
- **What they care about**: job security, practical utility, what this means for their day-to-day work
- **What they know**: general awareness of AI; mixed comfort levels

## Central Message

AI is here to make consultants better at their jobs — not to replace them. The goal is to show concrete, practical ways AI fits into the work they already do.

## Decision / Action

No decision required. This is an awareness update. Desired outcome: consultants leave with a clear picture of what the AI integration plan looks like and feel encouraged (not threatened) to adopt it.

## Deck Constraints

- Use open template (`.pptx`)
- Tone: confident, enabling, human — avoid hype and fear
- Must not imply AI replaces consultant judgment or relationships
- Keep slide count reasonable for a company update (aim for 10–15 slides)
