# Workshop Deck Outline

## Purpose

Guide a live group through a structured session that produces decisions, ideas, plans, or shared understanding.

## Default Flow

1. Session title and outcome.
2. Agenda.
3. Working norms.
4. Context.
5. Exercise 1 prompt.
6. Share-out.
7. Synthesis.
8. Exercise 2 prompt.
9. Decision or prioritization.
10. Commitments and next steps.

## Common Failure Modes

- Vague prompts.
- Missing timing.
- No artifact produced.
- Too much presenting, not enough doing.

## Design Defaults

- Facilitation-first.
- Use timers, prompts, canvases, and clear output formats.
- Speaker notes should include instructions, timing, and expected outputs.
