# Pitch Deck Outline

## Purpose

Persuade investors, partners, or strategic stakeholders to believe in an opportunity and take the requested next step.

## Default Flow

1. Title / sharp promise.
2. Problem.
3. Cost of the problem.
4. Insight / why now.
5. Solution.
6. Product or approach.
7. Market.
8. Traction or proof.
9. Business model.
10. Go-to-market.
11. Competition / differentiation.
12. Team.
13. Ask.

## Common Failure Modes

- Inflated market claims.
- Too much product detail before the problem lands.
- Weak evidence.
- No specific ask.

## Design Defaults

- Confident, concise, evidence-led.
- Use bold section pacing and proof slides.
- Avoid generic startup gradient aesthetics unless brand requires it.
