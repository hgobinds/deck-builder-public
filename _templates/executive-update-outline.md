# Executive Update Outline

## Purpose

Brief leaders on status, risk, recommendation, or decision with minimum noise and maximum consequence.

## Default Flow

1. Executive headline.
2. What changed.
3. Current state.
4. Key evidence.
5. Implications.
6. Options or trade-offs.
7. Recommendation.
8. Decision needed.
9. Next steps.

## Common Failure Modes

- Reporting activity instead of consequence.
- No clear decision.
- Too many details before the headline.
- Unqualified risk.

## Design Defaults

- Direct, structured, and decision-led.
- Use tables, charts, issue trees, and recommendation slides.
- Keep appendices separate when detail is needed.
