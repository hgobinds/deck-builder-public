# Training Deck Outline

## Purpose

Teach a skill, process, tool, or behavior with enough practice and reinforcement for the learner to use it.

## Default Flow

1. Learning goal.
2. Why it matters.
3. Mental model or concept.
4. Worked example.
5. Practice prompt.
6. Reflection or discussion.
7. Common mistakes.
8. Recap.
9. Next action.

## Common Failure Modes

- Too much explanation.
- Too little practice.
- Missing examples.
- No clear learner output.

## Design Defaults

- Clean instructional pacing.
- Use examples, exercises, and recap slides.
- Speaker notes should include facilitation cues.
