# QA Checklist

Use this checklist during Stage 07.

## Narrative

- Does the deck make sense slide by slide?
- Does each slide have one job?
- Does the close match the requested decision or action?

## Text

- Any typos?
- Any filler or generic wording?
- Any inconsistent terminology?
- Do slide titles carry the message?

## Visual

- Any overlaps, cutoffs, or cramped spacing?
- Are margins and alignment consistent?
- Is contrast readable?
- Are charts legible?
- Are slide thumbnails visually coherent?

## Source And Hallucination Audit

- Does every factual claim trace to approved source material?
- Do numbers, dates, names, and quotes match saved sources?
- Are rejected or undecided sources absent from the deck?
- Are weak claims qualified or removed?

## Template Hygiene

- No lorem ipsum.
- No placeholder text.
- No unused shapes or wrong logos.
- No stale client names.

## Speaker Readiness

- Speaker notes match the slide sequence.
- Notes do not introduce unsupported claims.
- Presenter cues are clear where needed.

## Final Pass

- Text extraction reviewed.
- Rendered slides or thumbnails reviewed.
- Fixes completed or explicitly tracked.
