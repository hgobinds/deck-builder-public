# Audience Profiles

Use this for stable audience patterns. Update only when a pattern is likely to matter across future decks.

## Investors

- Care about: market, traction, defensibility, team, return profile.
- Need: concise proof and credible assumptions.
- Skeptical of: inflated TAM, vague moat, unverified traction.

## Executives

- Care about: decision, trade-offs, risk, timing, operational impact.
- Need: clear recommendation and implications.
- Skeptical of: detail without consequence, unsupported certainty.

## Buyers

- Care about: problem fit, value, trust, implementation risk.
- Need: clear before/after and proof.
- Skeptical of: generic benefits and weak differentiation.

## Employees

- Care about: why this matters, what changes, what they need to do.
- Need: clarity, context, and practical next steps.
- Skeptical of: abstract strategy with no operational meaning.

## Workshop Participants

- Care about: flow, participation, relevance, usable outputs.
- Need: exercises, prompts, facilitation structure.
- Skeptical of: lectures disguised as workshops.

## Technical Evaluators

- Care about: architecture, constraints, integration, security, reliability.
- Need: specificity and evidence.
- Skeptical of: vague capability claims and missing edge cases.
