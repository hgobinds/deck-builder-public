# Deck Types

Use this file to choose the default structure and failure modes for each deck.

## Pitch Deck

- Purpose: persuade investors, partners, or stakeholders to believe in an opportunity.
- Typical length: 10-15 slides.
- Required sections: problem, insight, solution, market, traction, model, go-to-market, team, ask.
- Common failure modes: too much product detail, weak proof, no sharp ask.
- Strong close pattern: why now, why us, what we need.

## Sales Deck

- Purpose: help a buyer understand fit, value, proof, and next step.
- Typical length: 8-15 slides.
- Required sections: buyer problem, cost of status quo, solution, proof, implementation, next step.
- Common failure modes: generic positioning, too much company-first content.
- Strong close pattern: recommended next step with buyer-specific value.

## Proposal Deck

- Purpose: turn scope and approach into a decision-ready package.
- Typical length: 8-20 slides.
- Required sections: context, objectives, approach, timeline, team, investment, terms, next steps.
- Common failure modes: unclear scope, buried assumptions, vague acceptance criteria.
- Strong close pattern: decision summary and approval path.

## Training Deck

- Purpose: teach a skill, process, tool, or behavior.
- Typical length: varies by session.
- Required sections: learning goal, context, concept, example, practice, reflection, next action.
- Common failure modes: too much explanation, too little practice.
- Strong close pattern: what to do next and how to know it worked.

## Workshop Deck

- Purpose: guide a live group through a structured session.
- Typical length: facilitation-dependent.
- Required sections: goal, agenda, working norms, prompts, exercises, share-out, synthesis, next steps.
- Common failure modes: vague prompts, weak timing, missing outputs.
- Strong close pattern: decisions made, owners, next actions.

## Executive Update

- Purpose: brief leaders on status, decision, risk, or recommendation.
- Typical length: 5-12 slides.
- Required sections: headline, context, current state, implications, recommendation, decision needed.
- Common failure modes: reporting activity instead of consequence.
- Strong close pattern: decision, owner, timing, trade-off.

## Board Deck

- Purpose: support governance, oversight, and strategic decisions.
- Typical length: 15-40 slides.
- Required sections: executive summary, metrics, strategic issues, risks, decisions, appendix.
- Common failure modes: too much operational detail, unclear asks.
- Strong close pattern: board-level decision and implications.

## Conference Talk

- Purpose: make an idea memorable for a public audience.
- Typical length: 15-40 slides.
- Required sections: hook, tension, story, insight, examples, takeaway.
- Common failure modes: information dump, no emotional arc.
- Strong close pattern: memorable line plus action or reframing.
